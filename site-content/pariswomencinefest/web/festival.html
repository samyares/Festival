About Us

Welcome to the Paris Women CineFest - Celebrating the Power of Women in Cinema!
Step into a world of inspiring stories and groundbreaking creativity with the Paris Women CineFest, an online film festival dedicated to championing independent women filmmakers. Our seasonal festival is a dynamic platform that spotlights the extraordinary talent and unique perspectives of women in the film industry.
At Paris Women CineFest, we are passionate about empowering the visionaries, the dreamers, and the trailblazers who are reshaping the cinematic landscape. Our festival is not just a showcase; it is a celebration of diversity, creativity, and the indomitable spirit of women in cinema.
We invite filmmakers, enthusiasts, and supporters from around the globe to join us in this vibrant online community. Through expertly curated film screenings, insightful panel discussions, and interactive events, we aim to foster a supportive network where women filmmakers can thrive.
Paris Women CineFest is more than just a festival; it is a movement. A movement to amplify voices, nurture talent, and inspire the next generation of women filmmakers. Whether you are a filmmaker eager to share your story, a film lover keen to discover new voices, or an advocate for women's empowerment in the arts, Paris Women CineFest is your destination.
Join us for an unforgettable experience that transcends borders and connects hearts. Be a part of the Paris Women CineFest and celebrate the transformative power of women in cinema!




Rules & Terms

1. Subtitles and Language: All films must include English subtitles unless the spoken language is English. Scripts should be submitted exclusively in English.
2. Submission Agreement: By submitting their films, participants consent to allow our team to screen their films online as part of the festival. This agreement also extends to any related promotional activities.
3. Originality and Rights: All entries must be original creations, and participants must possess all necessary rights to their content. Any instances of plagiarism will lead to immediate disqualification.
4. Entry Fee: An entry fee must accompany each film submission. Please note that this fee is non-refundable under any circumstances.
5. Eligibility: The Paris Women CineFest welcomes submissions from independent women filmmakers worldwide. Films that have already been distributed or publicly released are ineligible for submission.
6. Submission Format: All films must be submitted in a digital format. We do not accept physical copies.
7. Judging Criteria: Films will be evaluated based on originality, storytelling, direction, production quality, and overall impact. The decisions made by our judging panel are final and binding.
8. Duration: Short films should have a runtime of no more than 50 minutes, including credits. Feature films should exceed 50 minutes.
9. Notification: Filmmakers whose works are selected will be notified via email by the date specified on the festival’s official website. Failure to respond within the allotted time may result in disqualification.
10. Promotional Use: By submitting your film, you grant the Paris Women CineFest the right to use clips, trailers, and stills for promotional purposes, including but not limited to the festival’s website, social media, and press releases.
11. Withdrawal: Once a film has been submitted, it cannot be withdrawn from the festival program after the selection has been announced.
12. Liability: The festival is not responsible for any claims related to copyright, trademark, credits, or royalty infringements associated with the submitted films.
13. Amendments: The Paris Women CineFest reserves the right to make changes to these rules and terms as necessary. Any modifications will be communicated to participants in a timely manner.

By submitting a film to the Paris Women CineFest, participants acknowledge that they have read, understood, and agreed to comply with all the rules and terms outlined above.
