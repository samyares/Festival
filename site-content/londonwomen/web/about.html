About Us

🎬 Welcome to the London Women's Film Festival - Where Creativity Meets Empowerment! 🎥

Join us for an electrifying celebration of women in cinema as we bring together the finest talents from around the globe. Set against the vibrant backdrop of London, our online film festival and screenplay contest are poised to ignite your passion for storytelling and propel your cinematic journey to new heights. At the London Women's Film Festival, we champion the visionaries, the dreamers, and the trailblazers. We proudly stand behind women filmmakers, actors, writers, and all those who are actively shaping the landscape of cinema. Our platform is not just about showcasing films; it's about amplifying voices, fostering diversity, and nurturing talent. But that's not all! Our screenplay contest offers aspiring writers the opportunity to showcase their storytelling prowess and gain recognition on a global stage. With expert judging panels and invaluable feedback, we're committed to nurturing the next generation of cinematic visionaries. So, whether you're a filmmaker, film enthusiast, or simply someone who appreciates the art of storytelling, the London Women's Film Festival invites you to be part of something truly extraordinary!

 

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common queries about the London Women Film Festival. If you have additional questions, feel free to reach out to our team for further assistance.

How can I submit my film to the festival?

You can submit your project through the Filmfreeway website. The Submit via Filmfreeway button is available on the home page of our site.

What are the categories for the festival awards?

We have many categories in terms of genre (best horror film, science fiction, women's empowerment, LGBTQ, etc.), form (music video, micro shorts, etc.), and technical (best director, best actor, best actor, best cinematography, best makeup, etc.). All of these categories are separated for both feature and short films. You can also use the All Categories option to be considered in all categories for a lower fee.

Are there any certificates for award winners?

Yes we have both certificate and official laurel for winner films.

Do I have to be a woman to participate in this festival?

No. We accept any films with women producer or director, or any projects directed by men about women and their issues.
