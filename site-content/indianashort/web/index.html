The Indiana Short Film Festival invites filmmakers from across the globe to join us in celebrating the art of short films through our online platform. We are proud to offer a space that showcases a diverse range of genres, providing filmmakers the opportunity to present their unique visions to an enthusiastic and wide-reaching audience.

Our festival is dedicated to recognizing and honoring the creativity and talent of filmmakers of all ages and backgrounds. Each season, our esteemed panel of judges will review the top submissions online and select winners in various categories.

Winning films will be awarded official certificates and receive extensive exposure through announcements on our social media platforms and website. Join us in this exciting event to connect with other filmmakers, gain recognition, and celebrate the power of storytelling through short films.

Rules & Terms

1) The maximum film time is 50 minutes 2) It is possible to send the film for several categories and in this case your film will be judged in each category. 3) Not all the submissions are selected for the official competition. Our Judges are free to make their own choices and no answers nor feedback will be given about not selected films. 4) Films completed on or after Jan 1, 2015 are eligible for consideration 5) We accept films of all genres. 6) Submission in other than English must have subtitles
